Psi Chi is the international honor society in psychology, dedicated to recognizing academic excellence, fostering research engagement, and supporting professional development in the psychological sciences. Our chapter serves students who demonstrate strong academic achievement and a sustained commitment to psychology, while also supporting a broader community of students through Psychology Club.

### What you can do here
- Learn the difference between Psi Chi and Psychology Club  
- See upcoming events and the semester schedule  
- Read weekly newsletters and monthly feature essays  
- Participate in community discussion and submit ideas
