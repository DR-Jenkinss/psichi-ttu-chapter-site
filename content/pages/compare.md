## What is Psi Chi?
Psi Chi is the International Honor Society in Psychology. Membership is selective and recognizes students who have demonstrated academic excellence in psychology coursework, strong overall academic standing, and a commitment to the discipline.

## What is Psychology Club?
Psychology Club is an open, inclusive organization for any student interested in psychology. Membership is not restricted by major, GPA, or academic standing.

## How they work together at Texas Tech
At Texas Tech, Psi Chi and Psychology Club operate collaboratively. Many events are shared. The key distinction is eligibility, expectations, and recognition.
